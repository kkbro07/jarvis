Here is a sample application for counselor leave:

**Application for Counselor Leave**

To,
[Name of Supervisor/Head of Department]
[School/Organization Name]
[Date]

Subject: Application for Counselor Leave

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from my duties as a counselor at [School/Organization Name] for a period of [number] days, from [start date] to [end date]. This leave is necessary for me to attend to some personal matters that require my attention.

During my absence, I have made arrangements for [Name of colleague/alternative counselor] to cover my responsibilities and ensure that the counseling services continue to run smoothly. I have briefed them on my current cases and they are aware of the protocols to follow in my absence.

I will ensure that all my tasks are up to date and completed before my leave, and I will be available by email if any urgent matters arise while I am away. I will also make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I return.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could approve my leave request. If there are any concerns or issues, please do not hesitate to reach out to me.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
Counselor
[School/Organization Name]

**Leave Details:**

* Leave Type: Counselor Leave
* Start Date: [start date]
* End Date: [end date]
* Number of Days: [number] days
* Reason for Leave: [briefly mention the reason for leave]

**Contact Information:**

* Email: [your email]
* Phone: [your phone number]

Please note that this is just a sample application, and you should customize it to fit your specific needs and circumstances.
